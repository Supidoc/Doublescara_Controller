1/************************************************************
2 * @file motor.h
3 * @brief High-level motor control interface with emergency stop and freewheeling
4 *
5 * This module provides a high-level API for controlling stepper-driven motors
6 * in mechanical units (angle and revolutions). It wraps the low-level step
7 * generation and TMC2209 driver configuration modules and exposes task-safe
8 * operations for movement, state monitoring, and safety handling. Supports two-motor
9 * synchronized movement for SCARA applications, emergency stop functionality, and
10 * motor freewheeling mode for free-spinning operation.
11 * @author dg
12 * @date 6 Apr 2026
13 ************************************************************/
